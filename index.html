Common Questions

What does SCIFY Technologies do?

We build R&D solutions for sustainability and infrastructure challenges. Focus is on making tech that actually gets used, not just prototypes that sit on shelves.

Are you available for speaking events?

Yes. I speak at college events, IEEE chapters, and startup bootcamps. Topics include startup building, R&D commercialization, and student entrepreneurship. Email me with event details.

Do you mentor individual founders?

I mentor through IEDC programs and occasionally take on individual mentees. Best fit is student founders working on technical products. Reach out with what you're building.

What kind of projects are you open to?

Sustainability, clean energy, infrastructure tech, and EV systems. Interested in partnerships where we can bring R&D expertise and execution capability.

How can I work with SCIFY?

We're open to consulting projects, research collaborations, and partnerships. Email with your project details and we'll see if there's a fit.
